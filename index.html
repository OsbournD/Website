Marketing Manager Richard Osbourn

Summary of work

Richard Osbourn is the head marketing manager for the flooring company "V4 Wood Flooring", helping "bring nature inside". V4 mainly sells Flooring products, not only wooden and "Concreate" flooring, but also cleaning products and other items to aid in upkeep of the buyers flooring.
Richard's aim is to ensure that the products of the company are appealing to the target buyers through the use of brochures, websites, social media posts on different platforms, and many other forms of advertising.
As well as primarily being the Head Marketing Manager of V4, Richard is also extremely experienced in graphic design, aiding him in creating many different forms of visually appealing content to help market the flooring. Click the image below to view one of the many brochures that he has created:

Richard also aided in certain design aspects of the newly constructed "V4 World Design Centre", located in Horsell,Surrey. This building acts as the primary HQ for the business, containing a very large workspace, featuring multiple offices for employees as well as a large showroom to showcase products to buyers.
